‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my reading progress?

+

Navigate to the Reading Progress Tracker page, enter your book details including total pages, pages read, and reading speed. Click "Calculate Progress" and then "Save Progress" to store your data locally in your browser.

Can I access Readify on mobile devices?

+

Yes! Readify is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. All features are optimized for touch screens and smaller displays.

How does the book recommender work?

+

Our recommender uses your selected preferences (genre and book length) to randomly suggest books from our curated collection. You can save recommendations to your personal reading list for future reference.

Are the ambient sounds free to use?

+

Yes, all ambient sounds in the Reading Flow section are completely free to use. Simply click on any sound to activate it, adjust the volume to your preference, and create your perfect reading atmosphere.

Is my data stored securely?

+

All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We don't collect or store any personal information on external servers. Your reading progress, saved books, and preferences remain private on your device.

How can I request a new feature?

+

We'd love to hear your ideas! Use the feedback form on this page to submit your feature requests. We review all submissions and consider them for future updates.
